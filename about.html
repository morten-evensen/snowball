About Us

Smart Living is a company that creates beautiful small smart houses, that are area effective and eviromentally friendly.

Our mission is to be a part of the solution for Un's sustainability goals. Some of their main goals is: Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient and sustainable

We believe that some of these challenges can be solved by living smarter. By living smaller houses, more people can live in a city with the nescessary luxary that is proven to give better life quality.

By using smart solutions like solar power roofs and walls, the small house can actually generate more electricity than it uses!

This is enviromentally friendly and very cost effective for the residents.
